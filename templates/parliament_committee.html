Parliamentary Affairs Committee

The Parliamentary Affairs Committee is responsible for deciding the agenda of the Parliament meetings. It also server the purpose of amending and bringing up-to-date all rules, procedures, structures, norms, convention, codes-of-conduct, etc. as well as study all proposed changes in these rules and procedures and give its opinion on the same.

Finance Committee

The Principal aim of the Finance Committee is to manage the finances of the Gymkhana. It controls and supervises the expenditures incurred by the executive wings of the Gymkhana, viz. The Cultural Council, The Science & Technology Council, The Games & Sports Council and The Film & Media Council.

Emergency Committee

Emergency Committee represents the Parliament in case of any emergency since the Parliament meeting cannot be called up in short time.

Festivals Committee

Festivals Committee is responsible for all matters except financial matters related to festivals organized by Gymkhana. All preliminary reports related to festivals are submitted to this committee.

Nominations Committee

The Nominations Committee is responsible for nominating students for various institute bodies.

Hostel Affairs & General Welfare Committee

The Hostel Affairs & General Welfare Committee looks after the matters related to hostels and general welfare of students.

Security Committee

The Security Committee serves as a student representative body to the Institute Proctorial Board. It oversees security arrangements in campus at all times and address students’ concerns and issues regarding crime

Students’ Placement Committee

The primary aim of the Students’ Placement Committee is to express students’ opinion regarding placements and it acts as the students’ central advisory body for the Training and Placement Cell.

Web Management Committee

The Web Management Committee manages websites of Gymkhana, festivals and student related activities. It is also responsible for managing all content of website. Undergraduate Students’ Academic Affairs Committee It advices and assists the Parliament on matters related to the undergraduate academic program. Postgraduate Students’ Academic Affairs Committee It advices and assists the Parliament on matters related to postgraduate academic program.

Alumni Relations Committee

Alumni Relations Committee assists the Dean of Resources and Alumni Affairs in improving student-alumni interaction. It collects information regarding alumni and serves as a critical link between the Institute and alumni.

Grievance Redressal and Enquiries Committee

Grievance Redressal and Enquiries Committee addresses grievances of General Body and acts as a source for providing information to the general body.
